Morning Calmness Activity Guidelines

  • Begin your day by waking up 15 minutes earlier than usual to allow a calm start.
  • Find a quiet, comfortable spot with natural light or calming ambiance.
  • Start with 3–5 minutes of deep, intentional breathing. Inhale through your nose, exhale slowly through your mouth.
  • Engage in a short mindfulness meditation or body scan to ground your awareness.
  • Optionally, journal your thoughts or set a gentle intention for your day.
  • Stretch lightly or perform simple yoga poses to energize your body peacefully.
  • Keep digital distractions (phones, notifications) away until after your calm time.
  • End the session with gratitude—list 1-3 things you're thankful for this morning.
Take these few minutes each morning to reset, recharge, and reconnect with yourself.

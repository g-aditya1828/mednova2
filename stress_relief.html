Welcome to Your Stress Relief Zone

This space is designed to help you unwind, breathe deeply, and center yourself. Follow a few simple tips and try out the calming games below.

  • Take a break and breathe deeply—inhale for 4 seconds, exhale for 4 seconds.
  • Drink a glass of water to refresh your body and mind.
  • Stretch gently to release any tension in your body.
  • Unplug for a few minutes: step away from screens if you can.
  • Practice gratitude—think of one thing you’re thankful for right now.
